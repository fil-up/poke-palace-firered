## Problem

HM moves cannot be forgotten, which limits quality-of-life options (e.g., replacing HM moves without visiting specific services).

## Solution

Allow HM moves to be forgotten by default (e.g., via Move Deleter or party menu) by relaxing the HM-flag checks for move removal.
