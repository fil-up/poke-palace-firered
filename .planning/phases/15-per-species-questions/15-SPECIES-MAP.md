# Species-Location Mapping Document

## Overview

This document assigns each catchable species to a **unique primary location**. The goal is to eliminate species appearing in multiple locations, enabling per-species question banks.

**Total Target**: 151 Gen 1 Pokemon + expanded availability of:
- Trade evolutions (Alakazam, Machamp, Golem, Gengar)
- LeafGreen exclusives (Sandshrew, Vulpix, Bellsprout, etc.)
- Previously unobtainable (starters, fossils, Eevee family)

| Tier | Difficulty | Sections | Question Difficulty |
|------|------------|----------|-------------------|
